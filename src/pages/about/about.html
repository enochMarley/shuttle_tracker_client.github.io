See Your Current Location

Enable your GPS in your phone's settings, and allow the application to access your location on request.


View Buses At Bus Stop

Click the 'Bus Stops' button on the top right section of the map. Select your preferred bus stop from the list of options.


Get Travel Details of a Particular Bus

Click the icon of the bus who's details you want to get. A response will be returned. It might not respond if you don't have good internet connection
